My Projects

Economics Project: A case study on the negative externalities of consumption - Vunato rubbish Dump, Lautoka.

This project, titled explores how overconsumption and poor waste management have led to severe environmental and health consequences for nearby communities. It uses both primary research (surveys and observations) and secondary sources to assess the spillover effects, including air and water pollution, and diseases like dengue fever. The study recommends policy interventions such as fines, taxes, community education, and sustainable practices to internalize these externalities and improve public well-being.This project strengthened my analytical skills and taught me the importance of environmental impact assessment.

Accounting Project: Analysis of financial report of RB Patel and creating a detailed report.

This project provides an overview of RB Patel Group Ltd, a publicly listed Fijian company that started in the 1930s as a small family-owned supermarket and has grown into a national retail chain with twelve stores. It outlines the company’s mission, vision, core values, corporate governance structure, and principal business activities in retail, wholesale, and property management. The report also details the roles of the CEO, board of directors, and auditors, emphasizing RB Patel’s commitment to ethical management, customer service, and community engagement.This project, Improved my understanding of business finance and report presentation skills.
